Destino: The Ultimate Travel & Trip Planner

Destino is a comprehensive travel management application designed to streamline your journey from the planning phase to the final destination. Whether you are organizing a solo backpacking trip, a family vacation, or a weekend getaway, Destino provides the essential tools to plan, budget, and preserve your travel memories in one secure platform.

Core Features

Smart Trip Management Effortlessly organize your travel itineraries with our intuitive trip management system.

Future Trips: Schedule upcoming journeys with detailed itineraries, including dates, destinations, and planned activities.

Trip History: Maintain a digital log of your completed adventures for future reference.

Saved & Cancelled Trips: Manage potential trip ideas and keep track of cancelled plans without losing data.

Destination Discovery & Navigation Explore the world around you with integrated location services.

Nearby Places: Instantly discover tourist attractions, restaurants, and essential services near your current location.

Interactive Maps: Utilize advanced map integration to pin destinations and navigate routes efficiently.

Explore: Browse through a curated list of popular destinations and hidden gems to inspire your next adventure.

Budget Planning & Financial Tracking Take control of your travel finances with built-in budgeting tools.

Budget Estimator: Calculate estimated costs for transportation, accommodation, and activities before you travel.

Custom Budgeting: Set your own financial limits and track expenses to ensure you stay within your designated budget.

Travel Memories & Media Preserve the moments that matter most.

Media Gallery: Upload and organize photos and videos directly within specific trip folders.

Camera Integration: Capture moments in real-time using the in-app camera features.

Memory Timeline: Revisit your travel experiences through a structured timeline of your media and notes.

Travel Tips & Community Reviews Make informed decisions with community-driven insights.

User Reviews: Read and write reviews for destinations and attractions to help other travelers.

Travel Tips: Access valuable travel advice, packing lists, and safety tips curated for various destinations.

User Profile & Security

Secure Authentication: Protect your data with secure login and OTP verification methods.

Profile Management: Update your personal details and travel preferences easily.

Privacy Focused: Your location data and personal information are handled with strict privacy standards, ensuring a safe user experience.

App Permissions Overview To provide a seamless experience, Destino requires the following permissions:

Location: To identify nearby places and assist with navigation.

Camera & Storage: To capture and save trip photos and verify profile details.

Network: To sync your trip data and fetch the latest travel information.
